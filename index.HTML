WELCOME TO BMW OF TRI-CITIES
The luxury and prestige of BMW is accessible to discerning drivers and car buyers at BMW of Tri-Cities. If you are looking for more than just a mode of transport, BMW provides the sophisticated style, authoritative performance, and premium features you require.
Visit our dealership to explore the complete lineup of new BMW vehicles in depth. Our expert sales staff will show you exactly what makes these luxury sedans, coupes, convertibles, wagons, and crossovers so special. We also have an extensive inventory of pre-owned vehicles, including options from a number of other luxury automakers. If you want the best for less, this is the place to start your search.
Closing any deal is fast, simple, and transparent thanks to our customer focused financing team. That means you can get out on the road and start enjoying your new vehicle sooner. In order to keep that vehicle in perfect running order, rely on our service department to handle all your necessary maintenance and repairs.
Our state-of-the-art facility and expert technicians can work on any model, but we are especially adept at working on BMW models. In our hands, you know that your high-performance driving machine is being perfectly serviced. We also maintain a huge inventory of official BMW parts and accessories to help you keep your prized possession in pristine condition.
We further enhance our services by providing you with the kind of dealership experience you can’t find anywhere else near Yakima, Walla Walla, Spokane, Bellevue, or Seattle. Every aspect of our dealership has been designed to ensure that meeting your auto needs is convenient, comfortable, and complete. Work with us in person seven days a week, or contact us at any time to have all your questions answered in full.
